Top 5 Places to See in Goa

1. Baga Beach

Famous for its water sports, shacks, and vibrant nightlife.

2. Dudhsagar Waterfalls

A mesmerizing four-tiered waterfall located on the Mandovi River.

3. Aguada Fort

A well-preserved seventeenth-century Portuguese fort.

4. Anjuna Flea Market

A vibrant market offering everything from handicrafts to spices.

5. Basilica of Bom Jesus

A UNESCO World Heritage site known for its baroque architecture.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best way to travel around Goa?
Renting a two-wheeler or a car is the most convenient way to explore Goa independently.
Is it safe to visit Goa during monsoon?
Yes, Goa is exceptionally lush and beautiful during monsoons, though sea swimming is prohibited.
