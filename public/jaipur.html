Top Royal Landmarks in Jaipur

1. Hawa Mahal

The iconic Palaces of Winds, known for its lattice windows.

2. Amer Fort

A majestic hilltop fort offering elephant rides and lake views.

3. City Palace

Grand complex combining Rajput and Mughal architectural styles.

4. Jantar Mantar

The UNESCO heritage astronomical observatory built in 1734.

5. Johari Bazaar

Famous for traditional jewelry, gems, and Rajasthani textiles.
