Must Visit Places in Manali

1. Solang Valley

Perfect for paragliding, zorbing, and skiing during winters.

2. Rohtang Pass

The gateway to Lahaul and Spiti, offering year-round snow.

3. Hadimba Devi Temple

An ancient wooden temple surrounded by cedar forests.

4. Old Manali

Known for its cafes, local markets, and riverside vibes.

5. Beas River

Ideal for river rafting and relaxing scenic walks.
